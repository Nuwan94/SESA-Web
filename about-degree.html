B.Sc.(Hons) in Software EngineeringAbout

Faculty of Science, University of Kelaniya proudly announces the launch of the new B.Sc.(Hons) in Software Engineering degree program for students from the physical science stream. This is the first ever Software Engineering degree in Sri Lanka to be offered for a batch of fifty(50) students directly through UGC selection catering to a long felt need for software industry in Sri Lanka.

If you are a physical science student who has obtained at least “S” grades for Mathematics, Physics, and either Chemistry or Information & Communications Technology at the GCE A/L examination, you are eligible to apply for this degree program.

This degree program will help you to gain core software engineering knowledge and skills during the first two years of the program. The students can then specialize in the application areas of,

  • Net-centric applications
  • Mobile computing
  • Business intelligent systems
  • Health informatics
  • Digital games and animations
  • Business engineering

Join the B.Sc. in Software Engineering degree program offered by the University of Kelaniya and become a very much sought after professional software engineer in the global software industry.
