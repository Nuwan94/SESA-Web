Inter-University HackathonRealHack 2018

RealHack, is the inaugural Hackathon organized by the Software Engineering Students’ Association of University of Kelaniya in collaboration with IEEE Student Branch @ University of Kelaniya. The inceptive grand event of RealHack will take place on 15th and 16th of October at Sri Dharmaloka Hall, University of Kelaniya.

All undergraduates are eligible to be a part of this grand event and the teams registering for the hackathon will had to face a hackerrank competition. The best 15 teams shortlisted thereby, will have the chance to engage in the final phase of the hackathon, which is a 24-hours event, at the University premises. Winners of the event will be chosen by a panel of reputed judges.

Winners ReturnX (University of Colombo School of Computing)
1st Runners Up PHOENIX (University of Moratuwa)
2nd Runners Up CODEX (University of Moratuwa)
